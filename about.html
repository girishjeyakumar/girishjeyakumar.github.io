Borrowing Michael Scott's wise words, when I decided I wanted to create a website for myself "I knew exactly what to do. But in a much more real sense, I had no idea what to do." But "somehow I manage(d)" and here we are .

Hello, World! I am Girish Jeyakumar. I am a Software Engineer II at Microsoft, currently living in one of the most beautiful cities in the world - Vancouver, Canada. I admit that the tedious amount of rainfall does bother me but well, atleast I am not under 20 feet of snow like most of Canada during winter.

I am an undergraduate alumni of the Computer Science and Engineering department at the Indian Institute of Technology Madras - a top-tier engineering institute in India which has an acceptance rate of less than 2%.

I love travelling and exploring the world. I have moved around quite a bit during my lifetime, but my home has been and will always be Chennai, India. It is super hot and traffic kills, but I love it with all my heart. My cliched dream as a traveller is to go all around the world and fill my map with so many pins that one can no longer see any land!

Looonng way to go but I am working on it!

I love listening to and making music. I started out with the guitar back in college and I have branched out since then into entirely producing, mixing and mastering my covers. I am passionate and I put out music as often as I can.
